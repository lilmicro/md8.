When you wake up write down your dream, even if you didnt dreamed add your thought straight after waking up. Add image that could describe mood of your dream. It can be object you saw or if you dont remember or didnt dream it can be something you thought about when woke up. It can be also color, anything. When this will get as habit, youll be able to make analisys on your dreams.
